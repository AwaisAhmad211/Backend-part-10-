* Types of DBs :
There are two types of DBs :
SQL : In SQL data is stored in a managed form. It is stored in the form of tables which consists of row and coloumns.
NoSQL : In NoSQL data is stored in the form of objects. Therefore, it is simillar to Javascript.In this backend series we will learn about a very famous and efficient NoSQL database whic is called mongoDB.
